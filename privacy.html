3. Data We Collect and Why

a) Flashcard Ratings

What data is collected

  • Ratings provided by users for flashcards

Purpose

  • To improve and evaluate flashcard content and learning quality

Legal basis

  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)

The ratings are used only for evaluating and improving the flashcards and are not used for profiling or marketing.

b) User Identification

What data is collected

  • An anonymous user identifier (RevenueCat ID), or
  • An email address, only if the user chooses to sign in

Purpose

  • To associate flashcard ratings and usage with a specific user
  • To enable account-related functionality if the user signs in

Legal basis

  • Performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
  • Consent, where applicable (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)

Users can use the service anonymously. Providing an email address is optional.

c) Newsletter Subscription

What data is collected

  • Email address

Purpose

  • To send the newsletter

Legal basis

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)

The email address is used exclusively for sending the newsletter and is not shared or used for any other purpose. Users can unsubscribe at any time via the link provided in each newsletter.

4. No Further Use of Data

The collected data is not:

  • Used for advertising or tracking
  • Sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Combined with other data sources
  • Used for automated decision-making or profiling

Data is processed only for the purposes explicitly described in this policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes or until the user:

  • Deletes their account, or
  • Withdraws consent (e.g. by unsubscribing from the newsletter)

Anonymous data that can no longer be linked to an identifiable user may be retained for statistical or analytical purposes.
